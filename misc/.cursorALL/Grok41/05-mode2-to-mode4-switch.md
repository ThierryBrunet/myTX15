# Mode 2 ↔ Mode 4 Switch Override (per YouTube method)

Assign a 2-position switch (e.g., SB or SG).

## Mixes Configuration

### Channel 1 (Aileron)
- Line 1: Weight 100% Source Ail  Switch: !SB↓ (Mode 2 default)
- Line 2: Weight 100% Source Rud  Switch: SB↓ (Mode 4 – left stick horizontal becomes aileron)

### Channel 2 (Elevator)
- Line 1: Weight 100% Source Ele  Switch: !SB↓
- Line 2: Weight 100% Source Thr  Switch: SB↓ (Mode 4 – left stick vertical becomes elevator; reverse direction if needed)

### Channel 3 (Throttle)
- Line 1: Weight 100% Source Thr  Switch: !SB↓
- Line 2: Weight 100% Source Ele  Switch: SB↓ (Mode 4 – right stick vertical becomes throttle)

### Channel 4 (Rudder)
- Line 1: Weight 100% Source Rud  Switch: !SB↓
- Line 2: Weight 100% Source Ail  Switch: SB↓ (Mode 4 – right stick horizontal becomes rudder)

Adjust directions/reverses in SERVOS page as needed after testing.
